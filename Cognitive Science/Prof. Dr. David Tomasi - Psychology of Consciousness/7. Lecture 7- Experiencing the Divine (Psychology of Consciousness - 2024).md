#### A. Understanding the Suprachiasmatic Nucleus (SCN)

The circadian rhythm is essential for maintaining mind-body balance, and it's controlled by the suprachiasmatic nucleus (SCN) in the hypothalamus. This region regulates neural hormone levels, sleep, wakefulness, blood pressure, body temperature, and more.

#### B. The Role of Melatonin
